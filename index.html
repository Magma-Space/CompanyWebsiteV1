Magnetic Reaction Wheels

Our magnetically levitated reaction wheels eliminate ball bearings — a major contributor to satellite jitter and failures. Experimentally achieving a 20× reduction in microvibration, Magma's levitated wheels provide the stability required for laser comm, high-res imaging and more. Available 1 Nms and up.

95% vibration reduction  ·  TRL 5  ·  Tested at NASA

Vibration Isolation Platform

The Magnetically Levitated Isolation Platform (MLIP) mounts between the spacecraft bus and precision payloads. Delivering <5% transmissibility across the full disturbance spectrum up to 500 Hz — with under 40 W of power draw and zero mechanical contact points.

50% sharper images  ·  3× optical comm data rates

Control Moment Gyroscopes

Magnetically levitated CMGs deliver the rapid, precise torque required for highly agile spacecraft maneuvers. Enabling quick slewing, ultra-stable settling, and the pointing accuracy demanded by directed energy, proximity operations, and in-space manufacturing missions.

10× higher agility  ·  Ultra-stable settling

AI Autonomy Stack

The GNC layer sits atop our hardware foundation — delivering adaptive self-optimizing control, collaborative constellation-level learning, and seamless AI-driven autonomy. Already deployed in our reaction wheel, AI/ML integration has accelerated onboard data processing by 500×.

500× faster processing  ·  ML Data Fusion
